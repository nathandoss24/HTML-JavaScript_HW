Billy Costigan, a young and hot-headed undercover police officer in the South Boston Police Department, infiltrates the Irish Mob headed by the notorious Frank Costello. Colin Sullivan, a hardened criminal who was raised by Costello, has risen among the ranks as a mole in the police departments Special Investigation Unit. Both become aware of eachother, but neither knows the true identity of the other. Each double agent must win the race to expose the other if they want to keep their lives.
